# Malnutrition — Lecture 2 of 2

**Clinical Forms, Assessment, Management & Prevention**

Reveal.js slides for the undergraduate Community Medicine lecture on Malnutrition (Lecture 2).

- Live slides: https://drarunmitra.github.io/malnutrition-lecture-2/
- Lecture 1 (Spectrum, Magnitude & Causation): https://drarunmitra.github.io/malnutrition-lecture-1/

Covers forms of undernutrition (PEM) and classification, micronutrient deficiencies,
the ABCD framework for nutritional assessment, WHO 2023 management of wasting and
nutritional oedema (NRC / 10 steps / CMAM), and India's nutrition programmes (POSHAN 2.0).

Built with Quarto (Reveal.js). Source: `malnutrition_L2.qmd`.
